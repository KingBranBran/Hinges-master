On the Subject of Hinges

Make sure the module doesn't fly at your face!

  • Some of the hinges on the module are missing!
  • Order the hinges from 1 to 8 starting with the missing ones, going from the bottom right hinge and going counterclockwise.
  • The rest of the hinges are ordered starting from the first present hinge after the last missing hinge and going clockwise.
  • Use the top two hinges to find out the order to press the hinges, skipping the missing ones that need to be pressed.
  • Do this until all the hinges are off of the module!
